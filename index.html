About the Conference

Second International Conference on Pure and Applied Mathematics-2018 (2nd ICPAM) is a prestigious program with a motivation to provide an excellent platform for the academicians, researchers, industrial participants and budding students around the world to share their research findings with the global experts. Original research papers from the field of Pure and Applied Mathematics, Mathematical Computation, Mathematical Chemistry and Mathematical Physics are accepted. 2nd ICPAM, December 2018 will be held at SCSVMV, Kanchipuram, India on 17-19, December 2018.

About the University

Sri Maha Swami of Sri Kanchi Kamakoti Peetam had cherished a desire to revive Kanchipuram to its pristine glory as a centre of learning, as Kanchipuram is one of the most sacred cities in India and famous centre for learning Vedas, Sastras, Philosophy and all the branches of knowledge (Ghaṭikāsthānam) from the very beginning. To revive the lost grandeur, Paramacharya of Kanchi Kamakoti Peetam established Nyaya Sastra Mahavidyalaya to teach our ancient Sastras. During 1993, at the holy occasion of centenary celebrations of Paramacharya, with the blessings of His Holiness Sri Jayendra Saraswathi Swamiji and His Holiness Sri Sankara Vijayendra Saraswathi Swamiji, the university was inaugurated with the name of their illustrious Guru, Pujya Sri Chandrasekhrendra Saraswathi Swamiji. This Vidyalaya is located in a sprawling complex of 50 acres at Enathur, at a distance of about 4 kilometres from Kanchipuram.
The Department of Mathematics, established in the year 1994, serves with a dynamic mission to achieve excellence in both academic and research. The department offers B.Sc., M.Sc., and research programs. Department has 25 faculty members in which 15 are doctorates. The department strives to provide excellent teaching, encourage interdisciplinary research and service to students and teaching community in the application of mathematics to other disciplines.

Journal Publication

Call for Papers

Original research papers from research scholars, academicians, scientists etc., in the field of Mathematics, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, branches and disciplines related to Mathematical Sciences are invited for presentation at the conference. All accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings with ISBN.

Selected papers from the 2nd ICPAM recommended by the scientific committee will be further recommended for the publication in any one of the following prestigious International Journals at concessional cost.

  1. International Journal of Pure and Applied Mathematics, Scopus indexed journal
  2. International Journal of Engineering and Technology (UAE), Scopus indexed journal
  3. International Journal on Smart Sensing and Intelligent Systems, Scopus indexed journal
  4. Punjab University Journal of Mathematics,UGC Listed Journal
Submission Guidelines
  1. Abstract not exceeding 200 words.
  2. Full paper not exceeding 10 pages
  3. Font size and style MS word, 12 Times New Roman, A4 size, 1.5 line spacing.
  4. Mail your papers to: icpam2@gmail.com
